vehicles observed several vehicles in the roadway preparing to race at 8400 Forney Road. The officers
activated their overhead lights and all the vehicles fled the location except one. Officers approached the
vehicle and gave commands to the occupants to show their hands. The driver complied, but the
passenger B/M/19 suspect did not and made furtive gestures. One officer fired his weapon missing the
suspect. No one was injured.
