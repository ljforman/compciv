On Saturday, November 24, 2006, at approximately 6:05 A.M., uniformed patrol officers driving marked
vehicles were dispatched to a disturbance with a gun call at 7740 McCallum Boulevard. Officers
encountered the L/M/26 suspect described on the call and by a witness in the apartment complex and
gave verbal commands for him to get on the ground. The suspect did not comply and pulled a handgun
from the waistband of his pants. One officer fired his weapon at the suspect striking him two times.
The suspect was injured and transported to Parkland Hospital.
Suspect was armed with an .380 caliber semi-automatic handgun, 0 live rounds.
Suspect was charged with Aggravated Assault on a Public Servant.
No officer was injured.
One officer fired 3 rounds. Involved Officer: W/M 4 years service.
