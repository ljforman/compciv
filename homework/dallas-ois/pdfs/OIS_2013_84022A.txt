On Saturday, April 6, 2013, at approximately 2:10 A.M., uniformed officers were working off-duty, in the
parking lot of a business at 534 Sunset Avenue. Officers were notified of an altercation in a parking lot
across the street and as they went to investigate, they observed a L/M/23 suspect inside a vehicle
attempting to run over groups of pedestrians. Officers gave commands to the suspect to stop and exit
the vehicle and he failed to comply. The suspect accelerated the vehicle towards the officers and one
officer fired at the suspect.
The suspect was not injured.
Suspect used a vehicle as a deadly weapon.
