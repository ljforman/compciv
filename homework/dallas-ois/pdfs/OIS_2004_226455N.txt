On Wednesday, March 24, 2004, at approximately 8:00 P.M., a uniformed officer was working in an offduty capacity at 1750 Regal Row. The officer heard a car alarm and went to investigate. The officer
observed an employee’s car with the door open and two L/M’s by the vehicle. The officer made contact
with the suspects and directed them to stop. The suspects entered their vehicle and began to flee. The
officer observed the L/M/20 suspect in the right front seat reach under the seat and bring an object up
and toward him. The officer fired his weapon at the suspect. The suspects then fled the location.
One L/M suspect was pronounced deceased at Parkland hospital. One L/M suspect was injured and
treated at Parkland Hospital. The third suspect was not injured.
Suspect weapon was a Crossman Model 781 BB Rifle. A hammer was also recovered from the suspect
vehicle.
One suspect was charged with Burglary of a Motor Vehicle.
No officer was injured.
One officer fired two rounds. Involved Officer: W/M/ 29 years service.
