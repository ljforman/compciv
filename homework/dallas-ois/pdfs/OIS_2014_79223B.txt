On Friday, April 4, 2014, at approximately 9:30 A.M., employees at 1221 S. Lamar Street arrived at work
and found the W/M/26 suspect trespassing on the property. As the employees escorted the suspect off
of the property, the suspect pulled a knife and began stabbing one of them. A uniformed off-duty
officer exiting her personal vehicle nearby observed the assault and fired her weapon at the suspect.
The suspect was struck six times.
The suspect was pronounced deceased at the scene.
Three knives were recovered at the scene.
No officer was injured.
One officer fired 7 rounds. Involved Officer: W/F 16 years, 1 month service.
