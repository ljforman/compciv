On Monday, October 14, 2013, at approximately 12:05 P.M., uniformed patrol officers driving a marked
vehicle responded to a Major Disturbance CIT Mental Illness call regarding a W/M/53 suspect who had a
history of mental illness. Officers were informed the suspect was off his medication, acting violent, and
in possession of a knife. Upon arrival, the officers observed the suspect seated in a chair in the middle
of the cul- de-sac holding a knife in his right hand. The suspect stood up and one officer fired four
rounds at the suspect striking him one time.
The suspect was injured and transported to Baylor Hospital.
Suspect was armed with a knife.
The officer was terminated for violation of departmental policy and later indicted by a Dallas County
Grand Jury for Aggravated Assault.
No officer was injured.
One officer fired 4 rounds. Involved Officer: B/M 5 years, 10 months service.
