On Friday, March 15, 2013, at approximately 1:56 A.M., uniformed patrol officers in a marked vehicle
were on patrol when they observed what they believed to be a hand to hand drug transaction at 9100
Skillman Street. While investigating this incident, officers observed a B/M/21 suspect step from behind
a dumpster and point what appeared to be a gun at the officers. One officer fired his weapon at the
suspect missing him.
The suspect was not injured.
Suspect was armed with a BB gun.
