On Tuesday, December 27, 2011, at approximately 9:20 P.M., uniformed patrol officers in marked
vehicles were dispatched to a suspicious person call at 3700 Penelope Street. Officers approached the
B/M/29 suspect and another individual seated in a vehicle, who were the individuals described on the
call sheet. The suspect reached for a handgun as he exited the vehicle. One officer fired his weapon
striking the suspect 3 times.
The suspect was pronounced deceased at the scene.
Suspect weapon was a .357 caliber revolver, 5 live rounds.
No officer was injured.
One officer fired 3 rounds. Involved Officers: W/M/ 3 years, 8 months service.
