apartment at 7340 Skillman Street, #103, when a B/M/18 suspect forced entry into his apartment via
the front door. The officer confronted the suspect as he entered the residence and the officer fired his
weapon one time, missing the suspect.
The suspect was not injured.
The suspect was unarmed.
The suspect was charged with Burglary of a Habitation.
No officer was injured.
