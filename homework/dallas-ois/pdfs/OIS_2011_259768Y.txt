On Sunday, October 1, 2011, at approximately 2:30 A.M., uniformed officers working an off-duty
assignment in the parking lot of a club at 2000 Commerce Street, observed a B/M/21 suspect in the
passenger seat of a vehicle, shooting a handgun in the air. Officers approached the suspect and ordered
him to drop the weapon. The suspect refused and pointed the handgun at one officer. One officer fired
their weapon, but did not strike the suspect.
The suspect was not injured.
Suspect weapon was a revolver.
Suspect was charged with Aggravated Assault Public Servant.
No officer was injured.
One officer fired 8 rounds. Involved Officer: B/M/ 10 years service.
